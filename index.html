About Me

My name is Stuart Tiedemann, and I am an aspiring front-end web developer. I am a southerner at heart who loves to travel the world but always manages to wander home to the warmth of the south. When I'm not working, I enjoy traveling, skiing, fishing, kayaking and pretty much anything else outdoors.
